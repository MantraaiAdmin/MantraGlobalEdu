About Us

Who We Are

Mantra Global Education is a specialized international education consultancy headquartered in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. We partner with students, families, engineering colleges, and universities to create structured pathways from campus to global careers — with integrity, expertise, and measurable results.

  • Boutique model — one dedicated counselor per student, not a call-center queue
  • 700+ students guided to universities across 19+ countries worldwide
  • 25+ partner universities with verified course and fee data
  • British Council certified counseling with 10+ years of expertise
  • Former IDP counselor leadership with deep admissions knowledge
  • Pan-India Top 17 · Best Counselor — Silver Category recognition
  • Free first counseling — transparent process with no hidden fees

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted boutique study abroad brand — where every ambitious student receives expert, honest, and personalized guidance to access world-class education and build a global career.

Our Mission

To simplify the international education journey through our proprietary MANTRA Framework — delivering end-to-end support from career counseling and university admissions to visa processing, relocation, and long-term student success.
